< About Me >

Welcome to my personal website! I put this together to practise my web design and HTML/CSS skills, but also present my projects and experience in a way that's pleasant to read.

Some more about me: I'm currently finishing a dual degree in software engineering and economics at the University of Queensland in Brisbane, Australia.

I've always been curious about how humans make decisions, and especially how technology can help improve those decisions, so studying both economics and engineering was a natural choice.

In terms of work, my recent projects have been looking at applications of distributed ledger technology ("blockchain") in the financial sector. I'm excited by how new technologies like this can be implemented to solve complex business inefficiencies.

My biggest passion in life though is music, and I spend much of my free time practising the piano and playing in a local orchestra. Reading and travelling are two of my other main hobbies.

If you want to get in touch, please feel free to message me via LinkedIn or email!
