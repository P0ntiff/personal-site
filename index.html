Evaluating Feature Attribution Methods

My undergraduate honours thesis was in the area of interpretable deep learning.

I developed a software package to bundle together "attribution" methods - scripts that can explain the predictions of black-box neural network models in terms of input features. For example, these methods can visualise that a model predicting "rabbit" looked at the ears and eyes in making its prediction, by highlighting those areas on the input image.

My paper examined the effectiveness of these methods and came up with several ways to evaluate them on novel criteria.
